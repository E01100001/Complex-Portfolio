About Me

My name is Ebony Penson. I am the oldest of 3 siblings, missionary kid and honor student for 8 years in a row. Like Naruto, I'm a bit of an odd ball, have a tragic past and am determined to be different and prove everyone wrong. Believe it! I was adopted at age 10 and have achieved so many things its crazy! Some may call me miraculous. I'm a sucker for animals, especially cats. I always geek when I see a animal.


I am an artist: I draw, mold things out of clay, fix toys, customize shoes, and play 4 instruments by ear. I love to game and swim, and I like to cosplay. I have 2 cats and 2 fish tanks and my saying is "Go big or go home!" I wan to become a video game designer or video game tester.
